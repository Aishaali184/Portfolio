About Me

My technical expertise includes React.js, Node.js, Express.js, Java, Python, C++, JavaScript, and SQL. I have strong knowledge of RESTful APIs, object-oriented programming, database design, and responsive web development. My certifications in Python (University of Michigan), Web Development (FreeCodeCamp), and Database Design (Oracle Academy) strengthen my technical foundation. I am passionate about problem-solving, clean coding practices, and scalable solutions.

I thrive on solving complex problems and am always eager to learn new technologies. When I'm not coding, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects and exploring new creative outlets.
